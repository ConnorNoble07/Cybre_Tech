Intro-

Hello! Welcome to Cybre Tech Industries! We're happy to have you. To establish our goals, we first ask you to realize: Connor Noble was the sole creator of Cybre Tech, Connor has big plans for the company, and we haven't started doing buisness. Our goals: Become a large tech company, do research to help the world, and make things that are fun and interesting to play or watch. There is still much to do before we can bring our goals to fruition in the real world.


Future Projects (Games)-

Curupted Corner Saga-

The story of a business owner for a company called Cybre Tech Industries, named Connor J. Noble. After his daughter Willow's abrupt death, his son-in-law contracts the same thing that killed Willow, he funds the research of a project called: Thunder. The first test is used on Connor himself and creates an evil clone of him named Curupted Corner.


Dungeon Masters-

A man named Stevely goes through a portal, with no memories of where he came from. or why he's here. He just knows, he's on a mission. Hijinx with monsters ensues.


Watchers Guild-

After going through a mysterious portal, a man finds himself in a forest named "Hell's Forest" he meets people and joins an orginization known as "The Watchers Guild", set to shut down a mysterious group known only as WGP. This man has amnesia, and remembers nothing of who he was before Hell's Forest.


Warren Gillian Project-

The Warren Gillian Project is a series of games cronicalling Warren Gillian's experience through out Cybre Tech before, during, and after the Warren Gillian Project's failure.


Razz Dazzler: It's Magic, Darling-

Razz Dazzler, in a never before seen world, explores the magic running through his veins. This being a seperate Razz from a different universe. has no memories we've seen before. Two warring factions of magic exist in this new world: the Good, Mana magic, and the Evil, Valor magic. Razz will go on a journey where he goes to shrines to gather orbs of elemental magic, granting him new spells.


Rouge Cinder-

A Canadian paramilitary group known as Rouge Cinder is fighting against a evil force with unknown origins known as the Gauntlet of the Storm.


The Story of Dick Dent-

Following the life of an Navy veteran, his earlier years, and years after the Navy. This Navy vet is my Great grandfather, and is a true story, or as close to true as he'll say. (He likes to exagerate)


Age of Blades-

Planned Company Departments-

Game Studio (Cybre Games)-

The public facing passion project of mine, Cybre Games focus on designing games for ideas I've always wanted to see played and experienced by thousands to millions of people. While a focus on my ideas will be in place to start, the ideas of others will be a priority as well. The game department was the first invisioned, being the first goal of this company before P.F.W. was invisioned.


TV, Movie, and Animation Studio (Coggs)-

Our television studio will be called Coggs, and it will make many shows and movies, both animated and not.


Technology and Engineering (Cybre Space)-

While our vision involves games, it also involves the future of engineering and robotics, hence, Cybre Space. However, this means we have two divisions of Cybre Space: Robotics and Hardware.


Reasearch and Development (Project Flaming Wolf)-

The goal of P.F.W. is to make the world a much better place. We can see glaring issues such as hunger, thirst, useless wars, pointless violence, and more. It's time someone with money and power used it to stop the problems of the world, not make them worse. P.F.W. is the means to exactlly the thing I want, an improved world. While Cybre Games publicly apears as my passion project, this is truly what I am passionate about, fixing this world 8 billion people call home. While being the last department invisioned, it is, by far the most important department.
